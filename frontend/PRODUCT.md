# Blues Clues HRIS — Super Admin Portal

## Product Purpose
Internal command-line portal for Blues Clues superadmins to manage all HRIS tenant companies. Handles company provisioning, subscription lifecycle, renewal tracking, and system settings.

## Register
product

## Users
Single user type: super_admin. Operations staff, not end-customers. Power users who know what they're doing. Expect efficiency over hand-holding. Likely working on a desktop monitor in an office.

## Brand
Professional, authoritative, trustworthy. Belongs to a company called Blues Clues. Navy + teal palette established. Not a startup; a stable SaaS platform for HR operations.

## Anti-References
- Generic SaaS dashboards (Notion, Linear clones)
- Neon / dark-mode startup aesthetic
- Oversaturated gradients

## Tone
Direct. Functional. Quiet authority. No marketing copy.

## Strategic Principles
- Speed of task completion over visual flair
- Status clarity is critical (subscription statuses, company states)
- Destructive actions (suspend, provision) must be confirmed but not buried
